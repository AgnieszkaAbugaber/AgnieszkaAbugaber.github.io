Info

I am a graduate of the Psychotherapy School INTRA, recommended by the Polish Psychological Association. I was also trained in the United States, where I am a member of the New York Institute for Gestalt Therapy (NYIGT) and the Association for the Advancement of Gestalt Therapy (AAGT). Currently I am a member of the Polish Association for Psychoanalytical Psychotherapy (PTPP). I am also a staff member at the PTPP’s specialist clinic. I conduct psychotherapy in Polish and in English.

Offer

I conduct psychotherapy with adults, adolescents and children.
